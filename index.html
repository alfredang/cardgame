Welcome! Here's how to play

Compete with other players for scarce opportunities by playing queue cards. Use strategy to win the resources you need and score the most points!

✋

Your Hand

You have 5 queue cards numbered 1–7. Low numbers give you Priority (first in line), while high numbers give more weight in Ballots (lucky draws).

⚙

Each Round

Check the Opportunities tab to see what's available, then go to My Cards and tap a card. You'll auto-switch back to pick your target. Then hit Submit!

☆

Scoring

Win opportunities to earn points. Match your milestone objective for bonus points. Collect sets of the same category for even more. Most points at the end wins!
