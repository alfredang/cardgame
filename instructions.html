Quick Start (30 seconds)

The game in one sentence:

Each round, pick one of your numbered cards and play it on an opportunity you want to win. Best card wins. Most points after 8 rounds wins the game.

Each turn is 3 taps:

  1. Tap a card from "Your Cards" at the bottom of the screen
  2. Tap an opportunity at the top that you want to win
  3. Tap Submit!

That's it! The game handles the rest. You can learn the strategy as you go.

What Is This Game?

Imagine everyone in a city competing for limited resources — housing, healthcare, education, food. Some things go to whoever's first in line. Some go to the highest bidder. Some are decided by lottery.

That's this game. You have numbered cards (your place in the queue) and Time tokens (your budget). Each round, new opportunities appear and you decide which one to go for and which card to play.

The twist: everyone plays at the same time, and you can't see what others picked until it's too late!

2-5 Players

How to Play (Step by Step)

1. Join a Game

One player creates a room and shares the room code or QR code. Others scan/enter the code to join. When everyone's in, the host taps Start Game.

2. What You Start With

  • 5 Queue Cards — numbered 1 to 7 (random mix). These are your "plays."
  • 10 Time Tokens — your currency for auction bidding.
  • 1 Milestone — a bonus goal assigned to you (check the Score tab to see it).

3. Each Round

Every round follows the same simple pattern:

1.
See what's available

3 opportunity cards appear at the top. Each shows its name, type (PRIORITY, AUCTION, or BALLOT), and what you win.

2.
Pick a card & target

Scroll down to "Your Cards", tap one. Then tap the opportunity you want to compete for. Your choices are secret until everyone submits.

3.
Submit!

Tap the Submit button. Wait for other players to submit too.

4.
See results

Everyone's choices are revealed! Winners collect their rewards. New cards are drawn, and the next round starts automatically.

4. Game Over

After 8 rounds (7 for 4-5 players), final scores are calculated. The player with the most points wins!

Card Types

Your Queue Cards (Hand of 5)

These are your "plays" — numbered 1 to 7. You play one per round.

The number matters differently depending on what type of opportunity you target:

Card #Best ForWhy
1-3PriorityLowest number wins first place
4-5FlexibleDecent for all types
6-7BallotMore lottery tickets = better odds

After you play a card, it's discarded and you draw a new one. You always have 5 cards.

Opportunity Cards (What You're Competing For)

3 new opportunities appear each round. Each belongs to a category (housing, education, healthcare, transport, leisure, finance, food, tech) and offers rewards like:

  • Score points — direct points toward winning
  • Time tokens — more budget for auctions
  • Milestone progress — helps complete your bonus goal

Tap any opportunity card to see its full details.

Disruption Cards (Wildcards)

One is revealed at the start of each round (shown as a red banner). It changes the rules for that round only. Examples:

  • Double Reward — Everything pays double!
  • Queue Shuffle — Highest number wins priority instead of lowest
  • Time Tax — Everyone loses 1 Time
  • Clear Skies — No effect, play normally

Always read the disruption banner before making your move!

Milestones (Your Bonus Goal)

You're auto-assigned one at the start. Check it in the Score tab. It gives bonus points if you meet its condition by the end. Examples:

  • Homeowner — Win 2+ housing opportunities (+4 pts)
  • Diversifier — Win from 4+ different categories (+5 pts)
  • Time Lord — End with 8+ Time tokens (+5 pts)

Your milestone hints at a good strategy, but don't stress about it — you'll earn points either way.

3 Ways to Win an Opportunity

Every opportunity card has a colored badge showing how the winner is decided:

PRIORITY

Lowest number wins

Like a real queue — whoever has the lowest queue number gets served first. Play your 1s, 2s, and 3s here.

AUCTION

Highest Time bid wins

Pay Time tokens to bid. Highest bidder wins and pays their bid. Losers pay nothing. Don't overbid — you might be the only one competing!

BALLOT

Random draw (higher card = better odds)

Like a lottery! Your card number = number of tickets. A 7 card gives you 7 tickets; a 1 gives only 1. Use your high cards here.

Memory trick:

  • PRIORITY = play LOW cards (1, 2, 3)
  • AUCTION = spend TIME tokens
  • BALLOT = play HIGH cards (5, 6, 7)

How Scoring Works

Don't worry about memorizing all this — just win opportunities and the points will come! But here's how it breaks down:

Points from Winning

Some opportunities give points directly when you win them (shown on the card). Easy.

Milestone Bonus (3-7 pts)

If you meet your milestone's condition by the end, you get its points. Check the Score tab to see your milestone.

Set Bonus (collecting same category)

Win multiple opportunities from the same category:

  • 2 of the same category = +2 pts
  • 3 of the same category = +5 pts
Diversity Bonus (collecting different categories)

Win from many different categories:

  • 3 different categories = +3 pts
  • 4 different categories = +6 pts
  • 5+ different categories = +10 pts
Life Balance Bonus (+5 pts)

If you end with 3+ different categories AND 5+ Time remaining, you get +5 bonus points. This rewards balanced play.

Tiebreaker

If two players tie on total score, the player with more remaining Time tokens wins.

Example Turn

The situation:

Round 3. You have 8 Time. Your cards: 2, 3, 4, 6, 7.

Your milestone: "Homeowner" (win 2+ housing).

Three opportunities appear:

Clinic Appointment PRIORITY
Premium Apartment AUCTION
BTO Flat Ballot BALLOT

Your thinking:

  • Clinic (Priority) — Your 2 is pretty good, but another player might have a 1.
  • Apartment (Auction) — Costs Time. You want to save Time for later.
  • BTO Flat (Ballot, housing!) — Your 7 gives you 7 lottery tickets. And it's housing for your milestone!

Decision: Play your 7 on the BTO Flat Ballot

Great odds in the lottery, helps your housing milestone, costs no Time. Smart play!

Tips for Beginners

Match your cards to the opportunity type

Low cards (1-3) for Priority. High cards (6-7) for Ballot. Time for Auction.

Don't overbid in auctions

You might be the only bidder! Start with small bids (1-2 Time).

Read the disruption banner

The red banner at the top changes the rules each round. "Queue Shuffle" flips priority!

Save some Time tokens

Ending with 5+ Time gives a Life Balance bonus (+5 pts). Don't spend it all early.

Go where others aren't

If everyone fights over one opportunity, the others are free wins!

Don't stress about strategy on round 1

Just play a card on something that looks good. You'll figure out the strategy as you play!

Frequently Asked Questions

What if nobody targets an opportunity?

It goes unclaimed and is discarded. Wasted opportunity!

What if I lose? Do I get anything?

Some opportunities give a small "consolation" reward (usually 1 Time) to losers. Tap the opportunity to see if it has one.

Can I skip a round?

No, you must play a card each round. But you can intentionally target an opportunity nobody else wants for a free win!

What happens to my played cards?

They're discarded after each round and you draw a new card. You always have 5 cards in hand.

How do I know what my milestone is?

Go to the Score tab during the game. Your milestone is shown below the scoreboard.

Can the host end the game early?

Yes! The host has a menu button (three lines) in the top-right corner with options to end game, restart, or leave.

What if I disconnect?

The game saves your session. Refresh the page and you'll auto-reconnect to your game.
